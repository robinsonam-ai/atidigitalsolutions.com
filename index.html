Featured Technology: DANTE Audio Over IP

Audinate DANTE Partnership

ATI-Digital is proud to offer Audinate's DANTE (Digital Audio Network Through Ethernet) technology, the leading audio networking solution for professional AV applications worldwide.

DANTE revolutionizes audio distribution by replacing traditional analog and digital audio cabling with a network-based approach, delivering pristine audio quality with near-zero latency over standard Ethernet infrastructure.

DANTE Solution Benefits:

  • Transmit hundreds of audio channels over a single network cable
  • Uncompressed, lossless audio at up to 192 kHz sample rates
  • Ultra-low latency of 1 millisecond or less
  • Perfect digital fidelity with no quality loss
  • Plug-and-play setup with automatic device discovery
  • Scalable from small to enterprise installations
